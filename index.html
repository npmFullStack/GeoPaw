Our Story

Innovating pet travel since 2025

GeoPaw makes pet travel safer and easier with durable, pet-friendly GPS products. We rigorously test our technology in real-world conditions to keep your furry friends connected wherever adventure takes you.

Our Founder

Leading the way in pet tech innovation

GeoPaw was founded by Norway Mangorangca, a 3rd-year BSIT student from Opol Community College, along with his colleagues Raven Añabieza, Dave Joseph S. Baa, and Ian Vincent Madayag. Their passion for technology and pet safety drives the company's mission to create innovative solutions for pet owners.

Inspired by the bond between pets and their humans, the team combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of pet care to develop reliable and cutting-edge pet safety products.
